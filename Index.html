About Us

Where Passion Meets Perfection

Founded in 2012, AquaShine has grown from a single-bay car wash into the region's most trusted auto care destination. Our team of certified detailers treats every vehicle as if it were their own.

We use only biodegradable, pH-balanced products that are tough on dirt but gentle on your car's finish and the environment. Our water reclamation system saves up to 80% of water compared to traditional washes.

Our Story

About AquaShine

AquaShine was born from a simple belief: every car deserves expert care, and every customer deserves an exceptional experience.

Our team of 20+ certified detailers uses the latest techniques and eco-friendly products. We recycle up to 80% of our water, use biodegradable soaps, and are committed to leaving a smaller footprint while making your car shine brighter.

Frequently Asked Questions

A Quick Wash takes about 15-20 minutes. Deluxe Wash is approximately 30-40 minutes. Premium Detailing ranges from 2-4 hours, and our Ceramic Shield package typically takes a full day. We'll give you an accurate time estimate when you book.

Walk-ins are welcome for Quick and Deluxe washes! However, we recommend booking ahead for Premium Detail and Ceramic Shield services to ensure we can dedicate the necessary time and bay space for your vehicle.

Absolutely! We use pH-balanced, biodegradable formulas that are safe for all paint types including clear coat, metallic, matte, and ceramic-coated finishes. Our products are also safe for chrome, aluminum, and carbon fiber surfaces.

Our monthly plans include unlimited washes at your selected tier, priority scheduling, discounts on add-on services, and free air fresheners with each visit. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. No long-term contracts!

Our professional ceramic coating provides up to 12 months of protection when properly maintained. We include a care guide and recommend complimentary touch-ups every 6 months for Platinum members. The coating protects against UV rays, acid rain, bird droppings, and minor scratches.
